MadhyaPradesh Tourism

Madhya Pradesh, known as the “Heart of India,” is famous for its rich history, wildlife, and heritage. Popular attractions include the ancient temples of Khajuraho Group of Monuments, the Buddhist site of Sanchi Stupa, and historic forts in Gwalior. Nature lovers visit national parks like Kanha National Park and Bandhavgarh National Park for wildlife safaris, especially to see tigers. The state also offers beautiful landscapes, waterfalls, and cultural festivals
